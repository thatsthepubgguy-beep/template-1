Our Philosophy

Where French Tradition
Meets Modern Artistry

Nestled in the heart of the city, L'Éclat Doré was born from a singular obsession: the belief that dining is theatre, and every guest deserves a leading role. Our kitchen weaves classical French technique with the bold imagination of tomorrow.

Each season we reinvent our tasting menu, guided by the finest local producers and the rhythms of the earth. Nothing is rushed. Everything is intentional.

The Culinary Mind

Chef Élise Moreau

Executive Chef & Co-Founder

Born in Lyon and trained under three Michelin-starred masters, Chef Élise Moreau brings thirty years of culinary devotion to every plate. Her cooking is at once deeply rooted in tradition and fearlessly inventive.

After stages at legendary houses in Tokyo and Copenhagen, she returned to France with a renewed reverence for ingredient purity — a philosophy that defines L'Éclat Doré's identity to this day.

"The plate is a story. Every element must earn its place."
